Electricity

Electricity is one of the most common energy sources in our daily life. We should start from this first.
Here are some tips to save electricity:
  • Turn off lights when you leave the room.
  • Unplug electronics when not in use.
  • Set the A/C to 24°C - 26°C.
  • Switch your devices to power-saving mode.

Water

Reduce water usage not only reduce ecological impact but also reduce your water bills!
Here are some tips to save water:
  • Turn off the tap while you are not using.
  • Fix any leaks in your home.
  • Use shower instead of bath tube.
  • Reduce the shower times. Keep it within 10 mins ideally.
  • Use a dishwasher instead of washing dishes by hand.
